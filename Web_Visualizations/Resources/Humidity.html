Welcome to Weather Vizz

Project Overview

The purpose behind this project was to annalyze the correlation to weather conditions of different cities as you get closer to the equator. We analyzed data from over 500 cities and looked for correlations between 4 main types of weather conditions: Cloudiness, Humidity, Wind Speed, and Max Tempurature. The previews to the right will take you to the corresponding chart to view our findings, there is also a page with our source data for those who wish to conduct further analysis. Our hope is that you find this information usful in making any decions weather related.
